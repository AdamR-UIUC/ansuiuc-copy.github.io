 Trip to visit

The total trip will be 6:30am-4:30pm, but we are considering turning this into a weekend excursion in St. Louis or Weldon Springs as well, so if you want to go, fill out the form and submit a $5 deposit either in cash to an eboard member of WiN or through the venmo. (@WINatUIUC)

 Jim DuBay 
 Exelon 
 Pizza Session 
Tuesday, February 12
5:00-6:00 pm
106B1 Engineering Hall
Jim DuBay, the Director of Reactor Services at Exelon Generation, will be hosting a pizza session with our ANS section next Tuesday. Stop by to learn and ask any questions about Exelon Generation and the nuclear power industry. Pizza will be provided!

Bio:
Jim DuBay graduated from the University of Michigan with a BS in Nuclear Engineering and began his work within General Electric's Nuclear Division to design nuclear core codes. Since then, Jim has worked as a reactor technician, earned a MBA from CU Boulder, worked with Westinghouse, and Exelon Generation.

 WiN Summer 
 Conference 
 Tours 
 Planning 
Wednesday, July 31
WiN is hosting tours of NPRE at UIUC during the US WiN National Conference (mostly attended by professionals).

Because our ANS chapter hopes to host a conference of our own in the future, this is a great opportunity for younger members and new grad students to get involved and gain experience in handling conference logistics. Plus, it looks great as background info for the bios in our potential future proposals.

If you are interested in helping out, join the #nationalconference channel in the WiN Slack. Contact Alyssa Hayes if you need an invite.
