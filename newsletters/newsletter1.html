 Undergraduate/Graduate 
 Student Conference Opportunities 
 
The ANS Student Conference is from April 4-6 at Virginia Commonwealth University. In order to receive funding and logistics from NPRE and ANS respectively, you must be presenting research at the conference. Without research, you would have to pay your own way to the conference. If you're an ANS member and are hoping to attend and present research, we'll try to match you with NPRE research that would otherwise not be presented at the conference. If you're interested, either as an individual or a group, then check out a list of NPRE faculty research, and fill out the interest form. Anybody can receive funding as long as they have something to present, and we would like you all to come!
