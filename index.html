  • December 2018

    Natalie Gaughan

Her research is on neutron spectroscopy with a deuterated stilbene scintillator. This month, Natalie has attended all of the Student Conference Planning Committee and General Meetings, an Outreach Meeting, the visit from Brandon De Graaf, and the SWE Engineering Exploration Event.
